Hyundai i20 N Line - Full Review

Introduction

The Hyundai i20 N Line is a sportier version of the standard i20 hatchback, offering enhanced styling, improved performance, and a thrilling driving experience.

Key Features

  • 1.0L Turbo GDi Petrol Engine
  • 7-Speed DCT & 6-Speed iMT Options
  • Sporty Dual-Tone Alloy Wheels
  • All-Black Interior with Red Accents
  • Smart Touchscreen Infotainment System

Specifications

  • Engine: 1.0L Turbocharged Petrol
  • Power Output: 118 BHP @ 6000 RPM
  • Torque: 172 Nm @ 1500-4000 RPM
  • Transmission: 7-Speed DCT / 6-Speed iMT
  • Mileage: 20.25 km/l (ARAI)

Pros & Cons

  • Pros: Sporty design, peppy engine, premium interiors, great handling.
  • Cons: Slightly stiff suspension, limited rear-seat space.

Conclusion

The Hyundai i20 N Line is a perfect hatchback for enthusiasts who love sporty styling and dynamic performance. With its turbocharged engine and aggressive looks, it's a fun-to-drive car with modern features.
